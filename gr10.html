ABOUT THE CLASS


Description

This course is designed to challenge students to push their abilities to create and innovate. Prompted by one of three design challenges students will be encouraged to learn how to think outside-the-box and develop a sense of commitment and determination required to make innovative products and ideas.

Evaluation

Students will be evaluated on their effort to learn new skills and to commit to completing their project prototypes. Skills will be tracked and students will be given opportunities to aquire certified digital badges that demonstrate valuable accomplishments.

Expectations

---> Students are expected to follow all safety guidelines and demonstrate acceptable behavior while working in the Fab Lab and Design Studio at all times.
---> Students should expect to visit the LCC Fab Lab on occasion outside of class time in order to complete prototypes.
---> Students are expected to present projects and learning outcomes at the Design and Innovation Fair.
